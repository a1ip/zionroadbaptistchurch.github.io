Ministries and Fellowships

Sunday School

Zion Road has a thriving Sunday School ministry. Beginning at 10AM on Sunday Mornings, adults are led by Pastor Henry Juma in a study through Matthew, while the children are led separately in a lively Bible study time that includes Bible stories, singing, and prayer.

Sunday Service

At 11AM on Sunday mornings, the main church service begins. This time is characterized by true Biblical worship in the form of singing, prayer, and the preaching of a sermon.

Bible Institute

At 5:00PM every Sunday Night, Zion Road is led by Pastor Mestler in a Bible class that covers subjects that are relevant to the everyday Christian life. Currently, the course is covering world religions by comparing what the religion says to what the Bible says.

Wednesday Prayer Meeting

Every Wednesday night at 5:30PM, Pastor Juma conducts a meeting that focuses on prayer.
